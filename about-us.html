About Us



We are a group of highly motivated computer programmers who value equality and quality. In our eyes everyone has must have the right to have access to the toolsa and equipments that they need to make their dreams come to reality. We try to remove barriers and limitations of accessing to the goods and services by renting the items with much lower price to save money and effort for everyone.



Rent'emAll Goals/Objectives

Our web application will empower the items owners to not only save money on storage area but also earn money by renting out his/her not-immediately needed items and belongings for a specified period under a negotiable contract. In this case, the borrower also spends much less for being able to use needed item while the borrower also does not worry about maintaining and storing that item after he/she has finished using it. The person simply ships it back to the item owner at the end of the contract period. Customer will be able to purchase insurance for items based on their interests to be protected in case of unexpected damage to the item. Our customers save much more money on using similar items in comparison with brand new buyers who pay high prices plus tax.

Our customers save environment by decreasing waste production and increasing reusability of items.
